the project for appartment management sytem 
------------------------------------------------

user types

    1. superadmin - the user controll all level
    2. admin - the user can control all level except superadmin
    3. user - the user can only control his own level

    --the admin also known as landlord or owner of the property 
    --the user also known as tenant or renter of the property

operation for the users
-----------------------------

admin or landlord 
    1. add ,delete ,update  new appartment (under him)
    2. add ,delete ,update  new user(tenant)
    3. view the collected, pending rent ( under him)
    4. view the appartment details (under him)
    5. view the user details (under his appartment) with the deatils of appartment name and room  etc..
    6. add, edit, delete, approve the payment of the user 
    7. view the payment history of the user (under him)

user or tenant
    1.view his room and details 
    2.do payment (intially uplaod screen short)
    3.view his payment history
    4.register complaints
    5.view the status of his complaint
    6.view room rent aggriment 
    7.get contact deatil of caretaker or owner
